01 / Design Story

Connectivity should feel calm.

Routers are usually treated as infrastructure — hidden behind televisions, tucked into cabinets, surrounded by cables. AETHER ONE explores a different idea: a high-performance device designed as part of the home.

The form uses a quiet architectural silhouette, a flush status light, and one tactile control point. No clock. No emoji display. No constant visual noise. Just enough communication when needed, and silence when not.
